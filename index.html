Haoran (Steve) Zhang


I am pursuing the PhD degree at the College of Computer Science and Engineering, Harbin Engineering University (HEU), under the supervision of Prof. Shichang Xuan. I received my MSc degree from Northeastern University, in 2019 and BSc degree from Harbin Engineering University, in 2016. Before joining HEU, I was a software engineer in ByteDance from 2020 to 2021 and a research assistant at Cambridge University from 2019 to 2020. During my MSc study, I was interested in biomedical image analysis using artificial intelligence algorithms. Now, I am interested in blockchain technology and its applications, especially in:

  • Blockchain-based Federated Learning

  • Blockchain Security

  • Blockchain-based Web3
